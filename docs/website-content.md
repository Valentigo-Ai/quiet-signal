Log how you're feeling in 15 seconds, and let someone you trust know - without having to find the

**Section: What it does**
Three quick scales - pain, anxiety, and energy - logged in about 15 seconds a day. No lengthy
assessments, no streaks to keep up, no pressure. Just a quiet, honest record of how you're doing.

**Section: Share a status, not your whole story**
After logging a check-in, choose to send a short, plain-language message to someone you trust - a
partner, a friend, a family member. Nothing is ever shared automatically. You decide, every time.

**Section: Built with veterans and military families in mind - open to everyone**
Quiet Signal grew out of a need for a simple way for people living with chronic pain and PTSD to stay
connected to the people who care about them. Crisis support resources are country-aware and always
one tap away, never hidden behind a login or a paywall.

**Section: Your data stays yours**
Everything you log is private by default. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.
Export everything you've stored, or delete your account permanently, any time, from Settings.

## About

**Headline:** Why Quiet Signal exists

Living with chronic pain or PTSD makes a simple question - "how are you doing?" - genuinely hard to
answer out loud, over and over, to the people who care about you. Quiet Signal exists to make that
easier: a private daily check-in, and an honest, low-effort way to let someone know when you want
to.

It's not a diagnostic tool, a crisis service, or a replacement for professional care. It's a small,
quiet habit that can make it easier to stay connected on the harder days.

**Section: Who it's for**
Quiet Signal was built with the chronic pain and PTSD community in mind, including veterans and
military families living with both. It's open to anyone who finds it easier to log a status than say
it out loud.

**Section: Pro**
The core app - check-ins, journal, history, and crisis support - is free, forever, for everyone.
Quiet Signal Pro is an optional upgrade for people who want to share with more than one person, see
extended 90-day trends, or export a summary to share with anyone you choose.

## Support

**Headline:** We're here if you need us

**Section: Contact**
For questions about your account, data, or the app generally, email
**support@quietsignal.co.uk**. We aim to reply within a few working days.

**Section: Not a crisis service**
Quiet Signal is not a crisis or emergency service and cannot respond to urgent messages sent
through this contact form or email. If you're in immediate danger, please contact your local
emergency number, or use the Support tab inside the app for country-specific crisis resources
available any time.

**Section: Common questions**
- *Is my data private?* Yes - everything you log is visible only to you, except for the specific
  check-in messages you actively choose to share with a specific person.
- *Can I delete my data?* Yes, any time, from Settings > Delete account - it's immediate and
  permanent, not just deactivated.
- *Is Quiet Signal free?* The core app is free forever. Pro is an optional paid upgrade for extra
  sharing, history, and customization features.

## Notes for whoever builds this in WordPress

- Keep the palette consistent with the app: warm off-white background (`#F6F1EB`), terracotta
  accent (`#8A6552`), dark warm text (`#2B2622`) - avoid clinical blue/white, that's a deliberate
  brand choice carried through the whole app.
- If the theme supports custom fonts, Fraunces (headings) + Karla (body) matches the app exactly -
  both are free on Google Fonts.
- The app icon (ripple mark) is in `assets/brand/icon.png` in this repo - good candidate for a
  small logo mark in the site header/favicon.
